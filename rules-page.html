College Rules & Discipline

  • Students should be present in the college at 11:00 a.m.
  • Use of Mobile and Vehicle is strictly prohibited in college campus.
  • Students should wear proper uniform. Students without uniform will not be allowed in college.
  • Material for the uniform is available in the college itself.
  • It is compulsory for students to attend all the practicals, lectures and seminars.
  • It is compulsory for parents to attend the open house conducted after every examination.
  • If a student remains absent, he /she will not be allowed to attend the class without Parent's note.
  • Strict action will be taken against the student if the student damages the school or college property.
  • All exams are compulsory.
  • If there is change in Parent's mobile no. or address, student should immediately inform the college.
  • Parents should pay the fees on time.
  • Verities of haircut and colors are not allowed.

Rules Of Attendance

  • Monthly 75% attendance is Compulsory.
  • Every student must remain present for the Lectures, Tutorials, Practicals, Seminars and Examinations according to the time-table.
  • Conduct of students in the class rooms as well as in the premises of the School (Junior College) should be such as causing no disturbance to the fellow students or to other classes.
  • Students applying for the Certificates, Testimonials etc. and those who require Principal´s signature on any sort of document or application should first contact the office.
  • In case of illness, a student must apply for leave with Doctor's Certificate at the earliest.
  • Students shall do nothing wrong either inside or outside the Higher Secondary School premises that may interfere with administration and discipline.
  • No union or association shall be formed in the Higher Secondary School and no outside person should be invited without prior permission of the Principal.
  • No student shall collect money as contribution for picnic, trip, educational visit to some place, get together or for any other activity without proper sanction of the Principal
  • Students are expected to take proper care of the Higher Secondary School property.
  • The Higher Secondary School shall not be responsible for loss of articles of the students.
  • Students should submit to the office counter articles found by them.
  • Matters not covered by the existing rules will rest at the absolute discretion of the Principal.
  • As per the rules of the H.S.C. Board examination, forms of students having less than 75% attendance cannot be accepted under any circumstances as their terms cannot be granted.

Rules Of Indentity Card

Students must carry their Identity Card and must present the same for inspection whenever demanded. They will not be allowed to attend the classes, enter the Library or participate in the extracurricular activities unless they have Identity Card which can be inspected occasionally by the Higher Secondary School Staff.

Rules Of Examination

  • All the examinations are conducted as per the norms of the Mumbai board.
  • XI STD COMMERCE students have two Unit Test exams (25 marks each), two Semester exams (one of 50 marks and the other that is final of 80 marks). Project and Oral (for languages) carry 20 marks.
  • XI STD SCIENCE students have two Unit Test exams (30 marks each), two Semester exams (one of 50 marks and the other that is final of 70 marks). Practicals carry 30 marks for Physics, Chemistry, Biology and Math's subjects and Oral for languages carry 20 marks.
  • XII COMMERCE AND SCIENCE students have two practice tests one semester and two preliminary exams.
  • Practice tests of 40 marks each, semester of 70 / 80 marks and preliminary exams of 70 / 80 marks each.
