Hon. Shri. Ramsheth Thakur( Ex.M.P.),Hon. Chairman

  • A true teacher by heart, charismatic and visionary mass leader and an entrepreneur established.
  • The Janardan Bhagat Shikshan Prasarak Sanstha, Panvel in the year 1992.
  • A self-made man, wealthy yet simple, humble and companionate.
  • Dedicated his life to the service of people.
  • He is committed to his avowed principle - "What you received from society gives back in multifold".
  • A person who gave a helping hand to many while charting his own destiny.
  • He firmly believes that higher education has its roots in strong values with holistic approach.
  • He is a member of Managing Council, Rayat Shikshan Sanstha, Satara - one of the largest education institutions in India.
  • He is the Chairman of the Sanstha, who provide valuable guidance for the fulfilment of our goals and objectives.

Hon. Shri. Arunsheth Jagannath Bhagat

  • Shri Arunsheth Jagnnath Bhagat – President of Janardan Bhagat Shikshan Prasarak Sanstha , is a very calm and quiet person who belives that "The Highest Destiny of an Individual is to serve than to Rule."
  • Shri Arunsheth Bhagat is also the working president of Bhartiya Janata Party,Panvel Taluka.
  • Being a committed social worker, he is associated with various social, cultural and health activities for the benefit of the society.
  • He is loved by the people for his principles of fairness, equality, justice. Integrity,honesty and trust.
